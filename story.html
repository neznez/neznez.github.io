Short story club!

Together with some of my friends, I run a short story club! We've been meeting somewhat regularly for almost two years now, and have had many long and fun discussions about the stories we've read. Below is a list of things we've read so far. My good friend Cas also maintains a list for posterity, along with songs she feels fit well with it. If anyone who is not one of the regular participants is for some reason reading this page, we're always open for recommendations!
